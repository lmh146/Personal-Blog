시각

Seoul, South Korea

February 14

Why did you decide to specialize in cover-up tattooing?

The first start was a very coincidental opportunity. A client asked me for a small cover-up tattoo. I hesitated because I've never made a cover-up tattoo, but the client insisted that she really wanted to cover-up her existing tattoo. I accepted the request and successfully completed my first challenge. Since then, many clients have started asking me for cover-up tattoos. People who want to cover their existing tattoos or scars generally have bad memories of them. Cover-up tattoos often serve to heal such wounds. I am very proud of this.

JooJoo

Los Angeles, United States

July 29

Joojoo has been diligently refining her artistic style since she embarked on her tattooing journey in 2017. She draws inspiration form the vibrant hues found in nature, architecture, clothing, and other diverse forms. Her unique creations beautifully capture the essence of oil pastels, showcasing a rich tapestry of colors and a distinct textured effect on the skin.

Duda Lozano

Barcelona, Spain

January 21

This Brazilian artist has become famous for his tattoos that represent magnificent embroidered patches. As a tattoo artist he always tried to do something to differentiate himself from the rest of the artists, while doing what he likes most: realistic tattoos. A long time ago he had worked on the creation of embroidered caps and shirts logos, so he realized that he could mix the two things and achieve a really unique style.
